About Event

It is our first flagged event where the contestants are given a character by the judge, who will be referred to as the captain. The situation given is that the ship is drowning, but the captain has only one life jacket. The contestants as the given character, need to convince the captain as to why they deserve to be saved. The contestant that wins the competition will be given the ticket to the final showdown, where they will compete with the winners of the other flagged events to the title of Mr/Ms. Spectrum.

Terms and Conditions

1. In the time given, the participant should repent their character given and convince the judge to save them from the wrecking boat.
2. There are no language restrictions. Profanity is strictly prohibited.
3. This event is divided into two rounds. Top Contestants will have a face-off finale, and the Winner will be given a ticket for the Final Showdown.
4. Results will be decided by the Judge and it will be the final call.
