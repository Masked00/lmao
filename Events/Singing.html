About Event

Our first non-technical event, where the contestants will have a sing-off. The event has a total of two rounds. In round 1, the contestants will be judged based on the videos submitted prior to the event. Then the selected participants from round 1 will have to give a live performance in round 2 on google meet. The winners will be decided by our esteemed judges. Visit our Instagram page, @spectrum_2k21 for the detailed terms and conditions of the event.

Terms and Conditions

1. A total of 2 rounds will be conducted where the first round will be judged based on the videos sent by the participants to SPECTRUM2K21SRMRAC@gmail.com.
2. The video of the participant should first be uploaded to their G-drive and the link to it is then to be submitted to the above email ID with viewing access.
3. Solo and duet participants are allowed.
4. The time limit of each video or live performance shouldn’t be exceeding 3 minutes.
5. Participants qualified for the 2nd round will have to perform live in G-Meet.
6. Participants shouldn’t use karaoke but can use any instrument of their choice for the performance.
7. Exceeding the time limit will lead to disqualification. Time will be calculated from the start of the performance.
8. Results will be decided by the Judge and it will be the final call.
